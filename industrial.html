Industrial Update

Purpose:

This part of the program will analyze the current state of the city and apply the rules that pertain to the Industrial Zone s

Data Storage & Maintanace:

The datastructures used are basic structs and Vectors in C++. The datastructures used here are RegionState and Zone.

The RegionState variable is used to see what the city currently looks like and How many Workers are availble for the Industrial Zone s. Within the RegionState variable we have a 2D vector of Zone s. This contains all the data we need to calculate the application of the rules for the city. These variables are directly modified in this functionality as they are passed by reference and are available globally.

Functionality:

The main function that makes this functionality possible is IndustrialUpdate(). It also uses 2 helper functions that help reduce repetitive code, IsAdjacentPowerline() and IsAdjacentPop().

The IndustrialUpdate() function takes in a RegionState variable which has all the state data needed to start calculating the next iteration for the industrial Zone s. The changes are then stores in a temporary RegionState so the rules are only being calculated from the previos step and not the progess of changes currently happening. Then, at the end of the calcualtions, the original RegionState variable is modified to represent the new changes to the city.

INDUSTRIAL RULES:

  1. If a cell has a population of 0, 
     is adjacent to a powerline in the current time step, 
     there are at least 2 available workers,  
     that cell’s population will increase by 1 in the next time step 
     and the available workers are assigned to that job

  2. If a cell has a population of 0, 
     is adjacent to at least one cell with a population of at least 1, 
     there are at least 2 available workers, 
     that cell’s population will increase by 1 in the next time step 
     and the available workers are assigned to that job

  3. If a cell has a population of 1, 
     is adjacent to at least 2 cells with a population of at least 1, 
     there are at least 2 available workers, 
     that cell’s population will increase by 1 in the next time step 
     and the available workers are assigned to that job

  4. If a cell has a population of 2, 
     is adjacent to at least 4 cells with a population of at least 2, 
     there are at least 2 available workers, 
     that cell’s population will increase by 1 in the next time step 
     and the available workers are assigned to that job

  5. Each time stamp, increase the goods by the population of the industrial zone.
