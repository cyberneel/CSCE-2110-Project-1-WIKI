Pollution Update

Purpose:

This part of the program will analyze the current state of the city and apply pollution to all the Zone s

Data Storage & Maintanace:

The datastructures used are basic structs and Vectors in C++. The datastructures used here are RegionState and Zone.

Each Zone inside of RegionState keeps track of the pollution.

Functionality:

The main function that makes this functionality possible is PollutionUpdate(). It also uses the helper functions ZoneDistance() and PollutionTotal().

The PollutionUpdate() applies pollution to the adjecent Zone s based on each Zone 's population and decresing its effect as the adjacency distance increases. This decay in effet is taken care of with the help of the ZoneDistance() function.
